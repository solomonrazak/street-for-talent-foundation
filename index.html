What We do

At Street4 Talent, we believe that addressing population control, proper healthcare, unemployment or human rights starts from the arcade of education.

We work alongside communities, governments and partner organizations at many levels to address all aspects of basic, secondary and vocational education....We carefully select children from the classroom and on the street to sponsor them through both educational and vocational schools.

Where We Are

Today, the organization helps train street children who are above school going age as carpenters, dance instructors, hair stylist, and specialists in other fields

Quickly realizing that most children are expected to work in the home or sell on the streets because parents are not financially stable, Street4 Talent teams up with local business owners to schedule classes for the street children. In addition to its educational efforts, the organization also seeks to provide opportunities to participants to exhibit their innovations.

Mission

Street4Talent is an organization that believes each child deserves access to quality education. Our mission is provide financial aid for basic, secondary and vocational education to smart and intelligent underprivileged children

We pursue this mission through integrated, holistic commitment to provide good education for unprivileged children, enroll many street children in various areas of formal or vocational training and provide humanitarian aid for people in need.

Partner

Building a strong and firm foundation takes teamwork. At Street4Talent, we partner with like-minded organizations doing good work among the less privileged communities

Our partners, which includes organizations and individuals are crucial to our work responding to holding events, organizing feeding initiatives and throughout the capital.

We accomplish this by providing text books to school children, food and clothing to the street children and dance lessons to some talented ones who are interested in learning. All of these necessities bring great relief to these children.


Ongoing Project(Safe Water For All)

The Safe Water For All project, seeks to provide portable and safe drinking water for communities that do not have access to clean water.As part of our commitment to help build a sustainable economy, we at Street4Talent foundation have decided to engage in providing safe and accessible water to communities in the country. This includes, providing 15 water generators to communities in the Northern, Upper East and Ahafo regions in Ghana.


We are working toward this goal by partnering with local community members, business organizations, and individuals to help bring sustainable water systems to their communities. Your support can help us curb water crisis in the country, making it a healthy living for everyone
