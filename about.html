About Us

Street 4 Talent is non profit organization in Ghana that works towards formal and informal education for underprivileged children who are under difficult circumstances but are extremely intelligent and talented. We are an operational organization, that plan and carry out substantial projects to accomplish our objectives which requires a great deal of careful planning, communication, and local involvement for each project.

Activists

Founded in 2010 by Nana Kofi Boateng the organization is currently operating in Greater Accra with plans of extending to all 15 regions in the coming years. Our 10 member group consists of individuals who are geared towards building the educational values of the country and giving back to the society, by helping children whose parents are financially unstable.  At Street4Talent, we believe as citizens of the country, we are inclined to help elevate others to promote a sustainable community. Currently, the foundation’s operations are carried out by,  Two Directors, Deputy director, Administration Assistant/Secretary, Project Support Officer, Treasurer, and nine Members.
